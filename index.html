smart-document-scanner

A Flask + OpenCV web app that turns phone photos of documents into clean, scanned PDFs. Automatically detects document edges and corrects perspective, removes shadows and denoises the image, then runs Tesseract OCR. A lightweight classifier tells posters, invoices, and dense text documents apart and applies a different extraction strategy to each — including a fully offline, from-scratch extractive text summarizer (word-frequency sentence scoring, no external API).

PythonFlaskOpenCVTesseract OCR
ai-resume-screening-system

A Flask app that ranks resumes against a job description automatically. Handles text, PDFs, and scanned images alike — running an OpenCV preprocessing pipeline (deskewing, CLAHE contrast, adaptive thresholding) and Tesseract OCR on scanned input before extracting structured fields like skills, education, and experience. Scores each candidate with a hand-written TF-IDF + cosine-similarity matcher (pure NumPy, no scikit-learn) across skills, experience, education, and certifications, then ranks and shortlists candidates with CSV export.
